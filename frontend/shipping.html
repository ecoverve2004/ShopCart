Shipping Information

Processing Time

All e-commerce orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders are shipped Monday through Friday (excluding holidays).

Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. Use this to monitor your package's journey in real-time.

Delivery Confirmation

Signature may be required for orders over $500. You'll be notified if signature is required for your e-commerce purchase.

Shipping Restrictions

  • Some items cannot be shipped internationally
  • Hazardous materials have shipping restrictions
  • Oversized items may have additional fees
  • PO Boxes cannot receive certain shipments

Lost or Damaged Packages

If your e-commerce package arrives damaged or is lost, contact us immediately with photos and tracking information. We'll arrange a replacement or refund.

Shipping FAQs

When will my order arrive?

Delivery times depend on your chosen shipping method and location. Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days for domestic orders.

Can I change my shipping address?

If your order hasn't shipped yet, contact us immediately to change the shipping address. Once shipped, address changes cannot be made.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! Standard shipping is free on e-commerce orders over $100. Other shipping methods have additional fees.

What if I need my order urgently?

Choose Express or Overnight shipping at checkout for faster e-commerce delivery. These options are available for most items.
