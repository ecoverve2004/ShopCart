Frequently Asked Questions

E-Commerce, WooCommerce & Shopping Help

What is ShopEasy?

ShopEasy is a complete e-commerce platform powered by WooCommerce. It provides everything you need for online shopping - from product browsing to secure checkout and order tracking.

Is ShopEasy free to use?

Yes! ShopEasy is a free e-commerce platform. You can browse products, create an account, and shop without any charges. We make money through partnerships, not user fees.

How do I create an account?

Click on the user icon in the top right corner and select "Register". Fill in your details and you'll have access to your e-commerce profile, order history, and wishlist.

Is my data secure on ShopEasy?

Absolutely! ShopEasy uses SSL encryption and follows WooCommerce security best practices. All your personal and payment information is protected with industry-standard security.

What payment methods does ShopEasy accept?

Our e-commerce platform accepts credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through trusted payment gateways.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary by location and product. Standard e-commerce shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping is available for faster delivery.

What is your return policy?

ShopEasy offers a 30-day return policy for all e-commerce purchases. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Contact our support team to initiate a return.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your e-commerce order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can track your package in real-time through our order tracking system.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, our e-commerce platform ships to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination.

What is WooCommerce?

WooCommerce is a powerful e-commerce plugin that powers ShopEasy. It's the leading open-source e-commerce platform used by millions of online stores worldwide.

How does WooCommerce integration benefit me?

WooCommerce integration means ShopEasy has enterprise-grade e-commerce features, reliable payment processing, inventory management, and seamless order fulfillment.

Is ShopEasy built on WooCommerce?

Yes! ShopEasy is fully integrated with WooCommerce, ensuring you get the best e-commerce experience with proven technology and continuous updates.

Can I export my data from ShopEasy?

Yes, since ShopEasy uses WooCommerce, you can export your e-commerce data including products, orders, and customer information in standard formats.

How do I add items to my wishlist?

Click the heart icon on any product to add it to your e-commerce wishlist. You can view your wishlist anytime from your profile.

Can I save my payment information?

Yes! For security and convenience, you can save your payment methods in your ShopEasy e-commerce profile for faster checkout on future purchases.

How do I apply a discount code?

During checkout, enter your discount code in the "Promo Code" field. The discount will be applied to your e-commerce order total automatically.

Can I modify my order after placing it?

If your e-commerce order hasn't shipped yet, contact our support team immediately. We may be able to modify or cancel your order.

What if I receive a damaged item?

Contact our e-commerce support team with photos of the damage. We'll arrange a replacement or refund immediately as part of our customer service commitment.
