Returns & Refunds

Easy 30-Day Return Policy for E-Commerce Purchases

Return Policy

At ShopEasy, we want you to be completely satisfied with your e-commerce purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy.

Return Eligibility

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of purchase
  • Products must be unused and in original condition
  • Original packaging and all accessories must be included
  • Receipt or proof of purchase is required
  • Items must not show signs of wear or damage

Non-Returnable Items

  • Clearance or final sale items
  • Customized or personalized products
  • Items purchased on sale (may have restrictions)
  • Digital products or downloads
  • Items without original packaging

Refund Timeline

  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return
  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable
  • Return shipping is free for defective items
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method

How to Return an Item

Step 1: Request Return

Contact our support team with your order ID and reason for return

Step 2: Get Return Label

Receive a prepaid return shipping label via email

Step 3: Pack & Ship

Pack the item securely and ship using the provided label

Step 4: Get Refund

Once received and inspected, your refund is processed

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I return items purchased on sale?

Sale items can be returned within 30 days if they meet all return conditions. However, refunds will be issued at the sale price paid.

What if my item arrives damaged?

If your item arrives damaged, contact us immediately with photos. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.

How do I track my return?

You'll receive a tracking number for your return shipment. Use this to monitor your package's journey back to our warehouse.

Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

Yes! You can exchange items for a different size, color, or product within 30 days. Contact our support team to arrange an exchange.
